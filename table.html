Corner Conference State Track Qualifiers

This is the list of all the Corner Conference track and field athletes who qualified for the 2017 Iowa State Track and Field meet, which takes place May 18-20 at Drake Stadium in Des Moines. This list includes all the qualifiers for the boys and the girls. This list is from the lane and heat assignments from the IHSAA and IGHSAU, posted early Saturday afternoon.
